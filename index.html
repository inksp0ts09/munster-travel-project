Why Munster?

Munster, the bicycle capital of Germany

Munster, the bicycle capital of Germany, offers a haven for cycling enthusiasts. With safe pedestrian streets and a network of bike trails, visitors can leisurely ride around the city or venture into the countryside. The bike routes feature attractions such as 100 castles, the trail of the Peace of Westphalia, and scenic nature rides.

Munster, Germany, caters to all types of cyclists as the bicycle capital of the country. Cyclists can enjoy a leisurely ride around the city's safe pedestrian streets or explore the countryside on well-maintained bike trails. These routes feature attractions like 100 castles, the trail of the Peace of Westphalia, and scenic nature rides, providing a memorable experience for bike enthusiasts.
