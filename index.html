Why Munster?

Munster, the bicycle capital of Germany

Munster, the bicycle capital of Germany, offers a haven for cycling enthusiasts. With safe pedestrian streets and a network of bike trails, visitors can leisurely ride around the city or venture into the countryside. Choose a theme to your cycling exploration, such as 100 castles, the trail of the Peace of Westphalia, or scenic nature rides.

Munster, Germany, caters to all types of cyclists. Stay in the city and cycle around as you shop. Take a leisurely ride in self guided tour fashion. Or explore themed day trips within Munsterland.

Bicycle Tours

Amazing Bicycle Tours in Munsterland

100 Castle route

Start your quest with a bicycle tour of 100 castles. Choose your destinations from 100 castles over 960 kilometers. Cycle with confidence guided by the signposted route. Each stretch of the journey has lodging available within a days ride of the next town.

North, South, East, or West

Choose a course of North, South, East, or West for a bike tour an average of 6 days long.

Learn More

Friedensroute

Discover the Route of Peace through bicycle tours. Follow the footsteps of the horsemen of peace, guided by the modern convenience signposted bicycle routes. You'll learn along the route and find many relics from the period.

East or West

Choose a course of East or West and easily cycle in one-day stages along Friedensroute.

Learn More

Nature Route

Explore a bicycle tour of the natural beauty of Munsterland. Experience the landscapes and when you get hungry there are plenty of places to stop to enjoy regional treats.

North, South, East, or West

Choose a Nature Route for a day trip, fun for the whole family.
